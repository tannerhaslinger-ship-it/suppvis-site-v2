Redefining the Supplement Journey

Welcome to SuppVis, the world's first AI-powered supplement optimization platform designed to bring clarity to a chaotic industry. We aren't just another health app; we are the infrastructure that makes an entire category of wellness finally work for the individual.

Our Mission

To serve as the independent, data-informed health command center for everyday consumers, bringing clarity, confidence, and measurable value to a fragmented and rapidly growing market.

We believe that health-conscious people shouldn't have to play "pharmacologist" just to feel better. Our goal is to transform supplement use from expensive, unguided guesswork into a precision-driven system that delivers results you can actually see and feel.

The Problems We're Solving

The U.S. supplement market is a $70 billion+ industry, yet most consumers are still "spending in the dark". We've identified four critical failures that prevent supplements from working:

The Personalization Void

Most advice is "one-size-fits-all," ignoring your unique goals, lifestyle, and existing health profile.

Generic recommendations don't account for your individual biology, leaving you to guess whether advice applies to you. Most people guess wrong.

The Interaction Gap

Users often unknowingly combine supplements that interfere with each other or with medications, leading to safety risks or wasted money.

Without comprehensive interaction checking, dangerous combinations go undetected and synergistic opportunities are missed.

The Execution Trap

Even with the right products, users fail at the "when" and "how," missing absorption windows or taking compounds that cancel each other out.

Proper timing is critical for effectiveness, but guidance remains vague, contradictory, and impossible to implement consistently.

The Consistency Crisis

Without a system to support habit formation, many people quit before the biological benefits even materialize.

Supplements only work if you actually take them. Most users abandon routines within weeks, blaming the products when the real issue is lack of execution support.

We are beyond excited for you to experience SuppVis. The idea was born from a frustration we know most of you share: the supplement industry runs on marketing hype, not science. T

Tanner's Story

CEO & Co-Founder

I've always been obsessed with health, wellness, and performance, constantly testing what it takes to feel and operate at my best. In 2021, I was diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes. It was life-changing, but it never stopped me from living on my own terms. If anything, it sharpened my focus.

Living with Type 1 means performing the functions of a human pancreas every single day without any breaks. On average, people with T1D make 300+ more health-related decisions compared to someone with a functioning pancreas. Every meal, every workout, every supplement has to be calculated to keep my glucose in range. That level of metabolic awareness changes the way you think about your body.

It also made me acutely aware of how little reliable guidance exists for people trying to optimize their health with supplements and lifestyle. SuppVis is the tool I wish I had. We built it so that anyone, whether you're managing a chronic condition or simply trying to feel your best, can finally take full control of your health without the guesswork.

Connor's Story

COO & Co-Founder

Growing up, I was always drawn to understanding how things worked beneath the surface. The human body, the natural world, the systems quietly running everything we take for granted. That curiosity followed me into college, where I studied food science thinking I'd find answers there too.

Instead, I found frustration. I was being taught that the only viable way to feed a country was through artificial pesticides, synthetic fertilizers, and herbicides. Not because it was the only truth, but because it was the most convenient one. Nobody seemed interested in finding a natural path to the same outcome. That bothered me deeply, and I couldn't let it go.

So I started digging. One rabbit hole led to another, and eventually those rabbit holes led me to the human body itself, and then to naturopathic medicine. I found a philosophy that actually aligned with how I saw the world: treat the root cause, not the symptom. Don't hand someone a prescription to manage a problem indefinitely when the goal should be to solve it.

"In Naturopathic medicine, Vis refers to the body's vital force, the unique biological frequency that governs the balance between resilience and vulnerability. It's the force that either sets your limits or shatters them."

SuppVis was built on that same belief. That the right information, matched to the right person, is more powerful than any band-aid fix. This is that tool, built for people who refuse to settle for managing when optimizing is possible.
